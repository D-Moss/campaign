Voices From the Community

Real stories from neighbors, supporters, and community leaders.

“

My name is Derrick Zhang, and I’m proud to stand here in support of Shaun Griffith for Congress. I first met Shaun as my tax professional. What started as a professional relationship quickly grew into something much more. Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to truly get to know him — not just as an intelligent and capable professional, but as a man of character. Today, I’m proud to call him my friend.
Shaun is one of the hardest working people I know. He approaches every challenge with focus, discipline, and a commitment to getting it right. But what really sets him apart isn’t just his intelligence — it’s his integrity.
He is honest, even when it’s difficult. He is passionate about doing the right thing, even when it’s not popular. And he is selfless — always willing to put others first.
Shaun has always been a voice for the voiceless. He listens. He cares. And he believes that leadership is about service, not status. That’s exactly the kind of person we need representing us.
I fully and wholeheartedly support Shaun’s candidacy for Congress because I know his heart, I know his work ethic, and I know his commitment to doing what’s right for our community.

Derrick Z. , Client / friend

”

I owe my introduction to tax to Shaun. Honestly, I don't remember applying for the tax internship at his office, but when I was called for an interview, I decided to go. That single decision changed everything. Shaun gave me my first-ever job in tax, and he didn't just hire me for one season; he believed in me enough to bring me back every single tax season after that while I finished my degree at La Salle. That consistent support and real-world experience were invaluable.
That internship with Shaun was the foundation for everything that followed. It directly led me to where I am today, working full-time at KPMG. But more than just giving me a job, Shaun showed me what it means to be a true professional and entrepreneur. He's incredibly ambitious and knowledgeable, not just about tax, but about how to genuinely help people. He treats his clients and his community with real care and dedication. Supporting Shaun isn't just about politics for me; it's about supporting someone who has personally invested in the success of others. I am so happy to support him in this next chapter of service.

Vanessa G. Former Employee
